# Outreach Plan

## Objective

Land the first clients in Johannesburg and online by positioning the practice as a specialist in learning operations reporting rather than as a general analytics freelancer.

## Positioning For Outreach

Lead with:

- operational intelligence for learning organisations
- programme reporting and KPI clarity
- cohort, completion, and intervention visibility
- dashboards and reporting systems for training and skills delivery

Avoid introducing the offer as:

- generic data analysis
- software development
- freelance dashboard work for any sector
- LMS replacement consulting

## Warm Network Outreach

Start with people who already know the founder's quality of work or communication.

Targets:

- former colleagues
- operators in training and education-adjacent spaces
- NGO or programme contacts
- founders of academies or skills businesses
- advisory and community contacts in Johannesburg

Approach:

- send a short, direct note
- link the website
- mention one or two tightly scoped offers
- ask for introductions to people dealing with reporting pain

## LinkedIn Outreach

Use LinkedIn for both credibility and direct prospecting.

Actions:

- align headline and summary with the site positioning
- publish short niche insights each week
- connect with programme managers, operations heads, academic leads, and founders
- send short follow-up messages framed around reporting pain rather than capability lists

Suggested message angle:

"I help training and programme teams tighten KPI clarity and reporting visibility so managers can see cohort risk, completion trends, and interventions more clearly. I recently built a focused site around learning operations intelligence and would be happy to share the approach if this is a current pain point."

## Direct Outreach To Academies And Providers

Use targeted email or LinkedIn outreach for:

- private academies
- coding and digital skills academies
- training providers
- workforce development organisations
- private upskilling businesses

Email structure:

1. Mention a plausible reporting challenge in their environment
2. Position the consultancy as focused and practical
3. Link to the site
4. Offer a short review conversation

## Demo-Led Outreach

Use the site as the centrepiece of outreach:

- homepage for niche positioning
- services page for scope clarity
- who it's for page for relevance
- case studies page for proof framing
- method page for maturity and trust

The Bontle proof point should be used as evidence of operational intelligence thinking, not as overstated sector proof.

## NGO And Programme Reporting Angle

This is a strong early route because many organisations:

- must report internally and externally
- rely on fragmented spreadsheets
- need better intervention and outcome visibility
- lack internal analytics capacity

Message focus:

- improve programme visibility
- tighten monthly reporting structure
- make intervention follow-through easier to track
- support funder and leadership reporting with better KPI logic

## Public Sector And Funded Programme Angle

Lead with:

- delivery visibility
- cohort progress reporting
- completion and attendance clarity
- management-ready reporting packs

Avoid promising:

- large platform builds
- full digitisation programmes
- enterprise transformation language

## Using The Site As A Conversion Tool

Use the site in:

- direct outreach follow-up
- networking conversations
- proposal support
- LinkedIn profile links
- lead magnet follow-up when sharing the white paper

The site should make the founder appear focused, credible, and serious within minutes.

## 30-Day Action Plan

### Week 1

- finalise the site and strategy files
- update LinkedIn profile
- build a list of 30 to 40 target organisations
- prepare 3 outreach message templates

### Week 2

- contact warm and semi-warm network
- publish 2 niche LinkedIn posts
- send 10 direct outreach messages to providers and programme teams

### Week 3

- follow up with non-responders
- share the white paper with selected prospects
- book at least 3 exploratory conversations

### Week 4

- convert strongest conversations into review or audit proposals
- refine copy based on objections
- continue one insight post per week
